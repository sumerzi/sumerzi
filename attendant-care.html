Attendant Care Services

Our Attendant Care Services are designed to provide the personalized support you need to maintain your independence and quality of life. We focus on assisting you with daily living activities, ensuring your comfort, safety, and well-being.

Wholistic Home Care Services offers the following Attendant Care Services:

  • Personal Hygiene Assistance: We assist with bathing, grooming, dressing, and other personal hygiene tasks to help you feel your best every day.
  • Mobility Support: Our caregivers provide support with transferring, walking, and positioning to ensure your safety and mobility within your home.
  • Medication Reminders: We help you manage your medications by providing reminders and ensuring that you take them as prescribed.
  • Meal Preparation: We prepare nutritious meals according to your dietary needs and preferences, ensuring that you enjoy a balanced diet.
  • Light Housekeeping: Our caregivers assist with household chores such as laundry, cleaning, and organizing to maintain a clean and comfortable living environment.
  • Companionship: We offer emotional support and companionship, engaging in conversation and activities to enhance your quality of life.

For inquiries, please call us at 765-705-0802 or send us a message through our Contact Us page. We are here to support your needs and provide the care you deserve.
