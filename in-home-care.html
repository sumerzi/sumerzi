In-Home Care Services

We believe that the comfort and familiarity of your own home is important for your well-being. Our In-Home Care Services are designed to provide high-quality, compassionate, and respectful care from dedicated caregivers who prioritize your safety, independence, and overall quality of life.

Wholistic Home Care Services offers the following In-Home Care Services:

  • Personal Care: Bathing, showering, dressing, grooming, and toileting assistance to ensure you maintain your personal hygiene and dignity.
  • Medication Management: Reminders, administration, and monitoring to ensure you take your medications as prescribed, supporting your health and well-being.
  • Meal Preparation and Feeding: Nutritious meal planning, cooking, and serving to cater to your dietary needs and preferences, ensuring you enjoy every meal.
  • Light Housekeeping: Cleaning, laundry, and maintaining a clean living space to ensure a safe and comfortable home environment.
  • Transportation and Errands: Accompanying clients to appointments, shopping, and social activities to help you stay engaged and connected with the community.
  • Companionship: Engaging in conversation, shared activities, and emotional support to enhance your quality of life and reduce feelings of loneliness.

For inquiries, please call us at 765-705-0802 or send us a message through our Contact Us page. We are happy to answer your questions and provide the care you deserve.
